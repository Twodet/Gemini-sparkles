Welcome To ART ORO

The Best Jewellery Store In Kenya

Art Oro Jewellers has had the privilege of serving families since 1948 as a third generation Jeweller. We are renowned for providing quality jewellery that satisfies all requirements and for having a comprehensive selection available in Kenya. We offer a range of 18ct & 22ct Yellow, White, and Rose Gold jewellery, as well as a selection of Diamonds, Precious gems and Platinum pieces. There is something for everyone!

View Products
  • High-quality Jewellery

    ART ORO Jewellers is the perfect destination for customers looking for high-quality, timeless pieces. Our variety consists of 18ct & 22ct Yellow, White and Rose Gold, along with Diamonds, Precious gems, Platinum Jewellery and much more. Whether it is a precious gemstone or a simple bangle, ART Oro’s selection delivers impeccable craftsmanship, outstanding beauty, and lasting shine. With on-trend collections from traditional designs to edgy modern pieces, you can find jewellery that suits your individual style.

  • Customer service

    ART ORO Jewellers provides amazing customer service with a knowledgeable team eager to help customers find the perfect piece of jewellery for any occasion. Whether it is making sure that a piece of jewellery is the right size and material, or recommending something unique and special, the team at ART ORO are more than willing to go above and beyond, for their customers' expectations. With over 60 years of experience in the industry and a commitment to quality, you can relax knowing your questions and requests will be taken care of in an efficient and professional manner.
